    Preserving my false sense of security,  2025

    Single-channel video, colour, sound, 8:45, glass jar, apricot jam

    The Long Way, SET Lewisham, London

    A group exhibition with Akinsola, Katie Tindle, Johanna Deverdier and Becky Astonon the theme of slow working.

    We had been thinking about slow work, craft and tech, and efficiency versus painstaking care.
    The works featured in this exhibition reveal how stories, recipes and craft are passed down hand to hand, mouth to mouth, while … making… things… s..l..o..w..l..y…
